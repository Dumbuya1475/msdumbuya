UniGuide Platform

UniGuide is an innovative platform designed to assist students in making informed decisions about their career paths. It provides comprehensive insights into university courses, job opportunities, and the skills required for various fields. The platform simplifies the career decision-making process, especially for students influenced by societal expectations or limited knowledge about certain professions.

Features

  • Extensive course information, including descriptions, prerequisites, and potential career outcomes.
  • Insights into job opportunities and industries related to each course.
  • A dedicated section for skill-building resources and recommendations.
  • Interactive and user-friendly interface for easy navigation.

Tech Stack

UniGuide is built using the following technologies:

  • React: For building the dynamic and responsive user interface.
  • Vite: Ensures fast development and optimized performance.
  • Firebase: Provides user authentication and data storage for seamless interaction.

Impact

UniGuide empowers students by providing the tools and information they need to choose a career path confidently. It bridges the gap between aspirations and actionable steps, fostering better alignment with individual interests and market demands.
