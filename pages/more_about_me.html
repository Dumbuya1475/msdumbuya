My Story

Growing up in Sierra Leone, I witnessed firsthand the transformative power of technology and education. My fascination with computers began early, but it was the potential to create solutions that could impact my community that truly ignited my passion for software development.

In 2021, I took my first steps as a freelance web developer, creating sites for local businesses and artists. This experience showed me how digital tools could amplify voices and opportunities in my community.

By 2023, I enrolled in ALX's Software Engineering program to formalize my skills while simultaneously co-founding Tech Inspire SL to address the gap in tech education and mentorship for young Sierra Leoneans.

Today, I balance my development work with my mission to inspire and educate the next generation of tech leaders in West Africa. Every line of code I write and every student I mentor is part of a larger vision to create a more inclusive and innovative tech ecosystem in my country.
