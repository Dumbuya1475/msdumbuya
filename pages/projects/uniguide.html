UniGuide

A career guidance platform to help students explore tech & non-tech courses, skills, and career paths. A project by Tech Inspire SL.

Status: In Development

The Problem

Many students in Sierra Leone lack access to comprehensive career guidance, making it difficult for them to make informed decisions about their future. They often struggle to find information about different courses, career paths, and the skills required for various jobs.

The Solution

UniGuide aims to solve this problem by providing a centralized platform where students can access all the information they need to make informed career choices. The platform will provide detailed information about different courses, universities, and career paths, as well as a personalized recommendation system that suggests suitable options based on the student's interests and academic performance.

Key Features

  • Comprehensive database of courses, universities, and career paths
  • Personalized recommendation system
  • Information about scholarships and financial aid
  • Forum for students to connect with mentors and peers
