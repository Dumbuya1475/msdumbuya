NourishWise

A smart nutrition guide that helps you make healthier food choices with personalized insights and recommendations. A project from my ALX SE course in collaboration with my teammate Val.

Status: In Development

The Problem

Many people struggle with maintaining a healthy diet due to a lack of knowledge about nutrition and the difficulty of tracking their food intake. They often find it challenging to find healthy recipes and to stay motivated on their health journey.

The Solution

NourishWise is a mobile app that helps users to track their food intake, find healthy recipes, and get personalized nutrition advice. The app also includes a gamified system that rewards users for making healthy choices, which helps to keep them motivated.

Key Features

  • Food and calorie tracking
  • Personalized meal plans and recipes
  • Gamified rewards system
  • Community forum for support and motivation
